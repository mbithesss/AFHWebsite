Introducing AFH

A Web 3.0 Utility Token for African Society & Beyond

African Heritage is a cryptocurrency with a limited supply of 1, 411, 808, 500 tokens created to engage and provide society with utilitarian services.

The web 3.0 Utility Token

Fully capable web 3.0 crypto token informing the future of money. AFH supports smart contratcts, DeFi, staking, DAOs, Proof-of-Stake high scalability, and fast transactions for the metaverse.

Decentralised Finance

AFH supports DeFi features, including a decentralized governance DAO for stakeholder rights.
