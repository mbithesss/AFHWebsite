How to add AFH to your decentarlized wallet.

To add a custom token to a decentralized wallet, follow these steps:

  • Make sure the token is compatible with the blockchain that your wallet supports. For example, if your wallet supports the Ethereum blockchain, the token must be an ERC-20 token.
  • Obtain the token's contact address, symbol, and decimal places.You cna usually find this information on the token's website or through a blockchain explorer.
  • In your wallet, look for the option to add a custom token. This may be under settings, assets, or tokens.
  • Enter the token's contract address, symbol, and decimal placesm then save the information.
  • Your wallet should now display the custom token balance and allow you to send and recieve the token.

Note: The exact steps to add a custom token may vary depending on the wallet you are using. If you're having trouble adding a custom token, consult the wallet's support documentation or community forums.
